About me

I define myself as a passionate teacher, Fullstack developer, and UX/UI designer. My journey in the world of technology has allowed me to merge my love for teaching with the creation of accessible and engaging digital experiences.

Teacher

Fullstack Developer

As a Fullstack developer, I immerse myself in the art of programming and web application creation. My focus on accessibility drives me to create inclusive solutions that reach all users, regardless of their abilities.

UX/UI Designer

As a UX/UI designer, I seek beauty in functionality. Every design I create is based on a deep understanding of users' needs and desires, crafting intuitive and appealing interfaces.

I am dedicated to education, technology, and design, and my greatest reward is seeing how my work positively impacts people's lives. I am always excited to learn and share, so don't hesitate to get in touch!
